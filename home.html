Gurkha Finance


Gurkha Finance is an organization that makes loans to individuals and businesses. Unlike a bank, a we do not receive cash deposits from clients, nor do we provide some other services common to banks, such as checking accounts. Gurkha Finance makes a profit from the interest rates (the fees charged for the use of borrowed money) they charge on their loans. Clients secure their loans with Gurkha finance by offering collateral (by pledging to give the company a personal asset, or possession, of equal value to the loan if payment on the loan is not made).
